Our Craft

Every detail, considered.

◈

Direct Sourcing

We travel to the farms ourselves — Ethiopia, Colombia, Yemen. No brokers. Just relationships built on trust and fair compensation.

◎

Precision Roasting

Each batch is roasted in small quantities using a 1920s cast-iron drum roaster. Temperature curves are mapped by hand, never algorithm.

◇

Sealed at Peak

Beans are sealed within 4 hours of roasting with nitrogen flushing. What arrives at your door is the same cup we taste in the roastery.
